About me:

Starting in the fall of 2022, I will be an Assistant Professor of mathematics at Duquesne University in Pittsburgh, PA, USA.

Before coming to Duquesne, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Georgia (2021-2022) and at Dartmouth College (2018-2021).

I completed my PhD in mathematics in 2018 at Indiana University advised by Charles Livingston.

My research interests lie in the field of low-dimensional topology. In particular, I am interested in knot theory in dimensions 3 and 4.
