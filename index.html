About me:

I am currently a Limited Term Assistant Professor in mathematics at the University of Georgia.

Before coming to UGA, I was a John Wesley Young Research Instructor at Dartmouth College. I completed my PhD in mathematics in 2018 at Indiana University advised by Charles Livingston.

My research interests lie in the field of low-dimensional topology. In particular, I am interested in knot theory in dimensions 3 and 4.
